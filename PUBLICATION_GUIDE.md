## üåê Free Publication Platforms

### Primary Distribution (Recommended)

1. **GitHub Releases** (Already Set Up!)
   - Upload PDF/EPUB/DOCX after each render
   - Permanent, version-controlled distribution
   - Free hosting forever

2. **Internet Archive** (Long-term Preservation)
   - Upload to [archive.org](https://archive.org/)
   - Gets a permanent URL + ISBN
   - Free, immortal hosting
   - Academic credibility

3. **LeanPub** (Donation-Friendly)
   - Free to publish
   - Readers can pay what they want (including $0)
   - You keep most of the revenue
   - Professional ebook formatting

### Secondary Distribution

4. **Gumroad** (Simple Donations)
   - "Pay what you want" pricing (minimum $0)
   - Easy checkout process
   - 10% + payment processing fees

5. **Google Books** (Discoverability)
   - Upload for free
   - Appears in Google Search
   - Preview pages drive donations

6. **ResearchGate** / **Academia.edu**
   - Academic audience
   - Free downloads
   - PDF only
